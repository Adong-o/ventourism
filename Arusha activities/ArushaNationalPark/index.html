ARUSHA NATIONAL PARK

Arusha National Park, located just a short drive from Arusha city, is a treasure trove of natural beauty and biodiversity. The park is renowned for its stunning landscapes, which include the lush rainforests of Mount Meru, the serene Momella Lakes, and the striking Ngurdoto Crater. These varied ecosystems provide habitats for a wide array of wildlife, making the park a perfect destination for nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts.
